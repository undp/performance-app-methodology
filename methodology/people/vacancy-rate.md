## Calculation of Scoring

&#x20;Vacancy rate 15% or lower = 100, Every percent above 15% reduces the total score by 2 pts. For example, a vacancy rate of 23% equals a score of 84.

## Traffic Light System

| Traffic Light | Score |

| Green         | 85+   |
| Yellow        | 70+   |
| Red           | <70   |
